About Us

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer based in Maranello, Italy. The company was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939 as Auto Avio Costruzioni, and the first Ferrari car, the 125 S, was built in 1947. Since then, Ferrari has become synonymous with high-performance automobiles, racing success, and automotive innovation.

Our Mission

At Ferrari, our mission is to create exceptional sports cars that embody the spirit of innovation, performance, and luxury. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and design to deliver an unparalleled driving experience for our customers.

Our Values

    Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from engineering and design to customer service and racing.

    Innovation: We are committed to pushing the boundaries of automotive innovation to create cutting-edge sports cars.

    Passion: Our passion for cars and racing fuels our drive to succeed and deliver exceptional results.

    Heritage: We honor our rich heritage and racing legacy while embracing the future of automotive technology and design.

Company History

Ferrari has a storied history in motorsports, with numerous victories in Formula One racing, endurance racing, and other prestigious competitions. Our racing success has not only shaped our brand identity but also inspired the development of our road cars.
